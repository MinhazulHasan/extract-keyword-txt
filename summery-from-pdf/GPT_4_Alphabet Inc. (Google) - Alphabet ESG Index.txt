Alphabet has mapped its ESG disclosures to the SASB and TCFD frameworks, detailing its environmental, social, and governance efforts as of April 2022. The ESG Index includes metrics on energy consumption, water use, data privacy, user information requests, data security, diversity, product lifecycle, supply chain management, materials sourcing, and intellectual property. Alphabet's related disclosures are found in various reports and documents, such as the Environmental Report, Supplier Responsibility Report, and CDP Response. The company acknowledges the continuous evolution of its ESG initiatives and commits to enhancing its disclosures over time. The TCFD Index section outlines Alphabet's climate-related disclosures, governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics and targets, with references to the CDP Response and Environmental Report for detailed information.
