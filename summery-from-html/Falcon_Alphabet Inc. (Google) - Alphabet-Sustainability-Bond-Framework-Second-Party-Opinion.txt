Sustainalytics is of the opinion that the Alphabet Sustainability Bond Framework is credible and impactful . This assessment is based on: USE OF PROCEEDS The eligible categories for the use of proceeds Energy Efficiency, Clean Energy, Green Buildings, Clean Transportation, Circular Economy and Design, Affordable Housing, Commitment to Racial Equity, Support for Small Businesses and COVID-19 Crisis Response are aligned with those recognized by both the Green Bond Principles .
