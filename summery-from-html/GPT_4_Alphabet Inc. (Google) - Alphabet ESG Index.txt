Alphabet has mapped its ESG disclosures to the SASB and TCFD frameworks, detailing its environmental, social, and governance efforts as of April 2022. The ESG Index includes metrics related to Alphabet's hardware infrastructure, data privacy, advertising standards, data security, product security, diversity and inclusion, product lifecycle management, supply chain management, materials sourcing, and management of systemic risks. Alphabet's related disclosures are referenced throughout the index, pointing to various reports and policies where detailed information can be found. The company acknowledges the continuous nature of its ESG journey and commits to enhancing its disclosures over time. The TCFD Index section outlines Alphabet's climate-related disclosures, governance, strategy, risk management, and metrics and targets, with references to the CDP response and Environmental Reports for more detailed information.
